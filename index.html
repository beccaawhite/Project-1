WAR

A deck of cards is randomly divided between the computer and you. Click "Draw Card" to play your next card. If your card is larger in value to the computer's card, then you receive both cards and they will be added to the end of your deck & vice versa. If your card is the same value to the computers, then it is war! Three additional cards are pulled and the last card is compared. Whichever players last card is largest in value, will recieve all cards. The game ends when one player has all 52 cards.
