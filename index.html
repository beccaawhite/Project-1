WAR

To begin game, click "Deal Cards." The goal of the game is to win all 52 cards. Click the "Draw Card" to start each round - this will play the top card of your deck.
The players card largest in value will receive both cards. If your card is the same value to the computers, then it is war! Three additional cards are pulled and the last card is compared. Whichever players last card is largest in value, will recieve all cards.
